What is Shanti Loops?

Shanti Loops is a small village-based project near Mananthavady & Thirunelly. Instead of a big booking portal, we quietly connect guests to a handful of trusted homes and stays that we personally know.

You message us on WhatsApp with your dates, number of people and rough budget. We suggest one or two options, share photos and details, help you with route guidance and stay in touch while you are here.

No rush, no fixed packages. Just honest stays in places where time moves slower and you can hear yourself think.

Who is it for?

Short breaks

2–4 day trips from Bangalore, Mysore, Calicut, Kochi or Kannur for those who want trees, rivers, chai and silence.

Ideal for couples, friends, small families.

Work & think time

Week-long or month-long stays for remote workers, writers, designers, early-stage founders and anyone in transition.

Wi-Fi in select homes · Simpler, slower days.

Slow travelers

For people who like staying in one place, meeting the same tea-shop guy every day and learning the rhythms of a village.

Walks, bus rides, small routines – not checklists.

What can you do here?

Forest & hill walks

Early morning and evening walks with views of Brahmagiri, birds and mist.

River & stream sides

Sit by the Kalindi or small village streams, read, talk or do nothing.

Home food & tea

Simple Kerala meals, village tea shops, local bakeries and toddy spots (if you’re curious).

Quiet work & journaling

Use the silence to think, write, plan or just let your brain cool down.

How it works

1 · Start a WhatsApp chat

Tell us your dates, number of people, budget range and what kind of vibe you want (quiet, social, deep jungle, etc.).

2 · We suggest 1–2 stays

We check with our homestay & village hosts and come back with options, photos and clear pricing.

3 · You decide, we support

Once you decide, we guide you on routes, food options, local contacts and help you settle in when you arrive.

Ready to loop back to Shanti?

Drop us a message on WhatsApp with your dates and a short line about why you want to come. We’ll respond as soon as we can with honest options – no spam, no pressure.
