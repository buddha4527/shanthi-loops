What is Shanti Loops?

Shanti Loops is a small, heart-led project from a village near Mananthavady. We work with local homestays and village homes around Thirunelly to offer calm, honest stays – from 3-day breaks to month-long digital retreats.

Instead of a big booking engine, we keep it simple: you message us on WhatsApp, tell us your dates, budget and vibe. We suggest a place, help you with route guidance, food options, and local support once you arrive.

What you can expect

  • • Traditional Kerala homes, village houses, and simple cottages
  • • Home-cooked food, local cafés & toddy shop recommendations
  • • Support from local youth for rides, treks and basic needs
  • • Calm spaces to think, write, reset or work remotely

Full website with stay listings, photos, and packages is coming soon. For now, everything happens over WhatsApp.

Who is it for?

Short breaks

2–4 day trips from Bangalore, Mysore, Calicut and Kochi for people who just want quiet, nature and good food.

Work & think time

Stay a week or a month while you work remotely, write, design or plan your next move in life or business.

Slow travelers

For people who love to live like a local – walks to the river, chai from the same shop, and familiar faces.
